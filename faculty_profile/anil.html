Dr. Anil Kumar

Designation: Assistant Professor

Profile Summary

Dr. Anil Kumar is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical & Automation Engineering at G.B. Pant DSEU Okhla Campus, New Delhi, with over 20 years of combined academic and industrial experience.

His experience includes roles as HOD in reputed institutions and earlier industry experience as a Production Officer. He has authored a Springer book on biomedical materials for additive manufacturing and has published around 20 research papers in indexed journals.

Areas of Interest

  • Production and Operations Management
  • Resource Optimization
  • Composite Materials
  • Waste Material Management
  • Lean Manufacturing
