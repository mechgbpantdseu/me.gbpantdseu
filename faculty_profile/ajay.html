Dr. Ajay Lamba

Profile Summary

Dr. Ajay Lamba is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at G. B. Pant DSEU Campus, New Delhi. He has a total of 16 years of teaching and research experience.

He has published research papers in SCI and Scopus-indexed journals and has also contributed book chapters published by Springer.

Areas of Interest

  • Manufacturing
  • Production Engineering
  • Non-conventional Machining Methods
