Profile Summary

Dr. Anurag Dixit is presently working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical & Automation Engineering at G.B. Pant Govt. Engineering College, Okhla, New Delhi.

He has more than fifteen years of teaching and research experience. He works in finite element analysis of textile composites and large deformation of metals and composites at low, medium and high rates of loading.

His research finds applications in defence, aerospace, marine industries and metal forming processes. He leads a ₹35.75 lakh DRDO project, has published more than 30 SCI journal papers and is editor of 3 technical books published by Springer.

Areas of Interest

  • Finite Element Analysis
  • Textile Composites
  • Large Deformation of Metals and Composites
  • Defence and Aerospace Applications
  • Metal Forming
