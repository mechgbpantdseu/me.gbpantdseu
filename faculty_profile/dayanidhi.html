Profile Summary

Dr. Dayanidhi K. Pathak is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at G.B. Pant DSEU Campus, New Delhi, formerly G.B. Pant Government Engineering College. He received his M.Tech. in Production Engineering from MNNIT Allahabad with a Gold Medal for first rank, and his Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from IIT Delhi.

With over 17 years of teaching experience, his research focuses on additive manufacturing, biodegradable metals, next-generation bioimplants, conventional and unconventional machining, and powder metallurgy, applied to metallic implants, scaffolds, and surgical devices.

He has over 10 SCI-indexed journal publications, one granted patent, and serves as editor for the Springer book series Biomedical Materials for Multifunctional Applications. Dr. Pathak is also an active member of the Robotics Society of India and SAE India.
