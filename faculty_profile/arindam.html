Faculty Profile

Department of Mechanical Engineering

Dr. Arindam Kumar Chanda

Designation: Professor (MAE), HoD (MAE), Dean (Academics)

Qualification: B.Tech. (Production Engineering, B.I.T. Sindri), M.Tech. and Ph.D. (Jadavpur University)

Email: faculty@college.edu.in

Profile Summary

Dr. Arindam Kumar Chanda was awarded his B.Tech. degree in Production Engineering from B.I.T. Sindri, and his Masters’ degree in Production Engineering and Ph.D. from Jadavpur University. He has more than 20 years of experience in teaching, industry, research, and academic administration.

He joined G. B. Pant Government Engineering College in September 2014 in the Mechanical and Automation Engineering Department. Earlier, he worked as Assistant/Associate Professor at Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University/Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Gautam Buddha University, Sharda University, and NIFT.

He has more than four years of industrial experience in Production Engineering in heavy engineering industry and also worked as Assistant Director at AICTE for about four years. He has published more than 25 research papers in peer-reviewed journals and conferences.

Areas of Interest

  • Production and Industrial Engineering
  • Advanced Manufacturing
  • Robotics
  • Flexible Manufacturing System

Achievements and Contributions

  • Member in AICTE Expert Committees and several internal and external technical committees.
  • Life Member of Institution of Engineers, Indian Society for Technical Education, Indian Institution of Industrial Engineering, and Society of Automotive Engineers.
