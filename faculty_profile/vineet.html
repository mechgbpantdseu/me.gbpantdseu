Faculty Profile

Department of Mechanical Engineering

Dr. Vineet Kumar

Designation: Assistant Professor

Qualification: B.Tech. and M.Tech. (G.B. Pant University of Agriculture and Technology), Ph.D. (IIT Roorkee)

Email: faculty@college.edu.in

Profile Summary

Dr. Vineet Kumar has more than 20 years of teaching and research experience. He was awarded his Ph.D. from IIT Roorkee for work on mechanical and fracture behaviour of ultra fine grained 6082 Al alloy.

He has published more than 8 SCI research papers in reputed journals with good impact factor and has 2 published patents. He has received teaching excellence awards in 2007 and 2008.

Areas of Interest

  • Fatigue
  • Fatigue Crack Growth
  • Fracture Mechanics
  • Creep Behaviour
  • Mechanical Behaviour of Materials
  • FEM
  • XFEM
  • Cryo-rolling
