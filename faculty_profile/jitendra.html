Profile Summary

Mr. Jitendra Kumar has more than 9 years of teaching experience. He got selected through UPSC and joined G. B. Pant Engineering College in January 2016 as Assistant Professor in Mechanical and Automation Engineering Department with specialization in Thermal Engineering.

Prior to this, he worked in different capacities at SD College of Engineering Muzaffarnagar, MIT Moradabad, Shivalik College of Engineering Dehradun, RIT Roorkee, and Dev Bhoomi Group of Institutions Saharanpur. He has several research publications in reputed national and international journals and conference proceedings.

Areas of Interest

  • Thermal and Fluid Engineering
  • Machine Design
  • Alternate Fuels
