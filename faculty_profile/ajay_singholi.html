Faculty Profile

Department of Mechanical Engineering

Dr. Ajay K. S. Singholi

Designation: Professor, Dean (Research and Planning)

Qualification: B.Tech. and M.Tech. (Mechanical Engineering, Dayalbagh University, Agra), Ph.D. (GGSIP University, Delhi)

Email: faculty@college.edu.in

Profile Summary

Dr. Ajay Singholi received his B.Tech. and M.Tech. degrees in Mechanical Engineering from Faculty of Engineering, Dayalbagh University, Agra and Ph.D. from GGSIP University, Delhi. He has more than 17 years of experience in teaching, research, and academic administration.

He got selected through UPSC and joined G. B. Pant Engineering College in December 2014 as Associate Professor in Mechanical and Automation Engineering Department with specialization in Advanced Manufacturing Systems. He is also a visiting Associate Professor at the Asian Institute of Technology (AIT), Bangkok, Thailand.

Earlier, he worked at Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women, Delhi as Associate Professor in Mechanical and Automation Engineering for more than 12 years. He has extensive academic administrative experience, including curriculum development, training and placements, and examination-related work.

Areas of Interest

  • Advanced Manufacturing Systems
  • Hybrid navigation system
  • Robotic green AGV design
  • Electric Discharge Machining optimization
  • 4D printing process modelling
  • Smart materials

Achievements and Contributions

  • Selected for the Award for Teaching Excellence by the Indian chapter of Indus Foundation Inc. USA.
  • More than 30 international research publications in reputed national/international journals and conference proceedings.
  • Supervising two Ph.D. students.
