Faculty Profile

Department of Mechanical Engineering

Dr. Bhajneet Singh

Designation: Assistant Professor / Head, Department of Mechanical Engineering

Qualification: Ph.D. (IIT Kharagpur)

Email: faculty@college.edu.in

Profile Summary

Dr. Bhajneet Singh earned his Ph.D. degree from IIT Kharagpur in 2016 and has over 15 years of teaching experience at both graduate and undergraduate levels.

His contributions include publications in prestigious international journals and conferences. He has guided and supervised numerous B.Tech. projects, with project outcomes featured in respected international conferences.

Areas of Interest

  • Computational Fluid Dynamics
  • Microfluidics
  • Convective Heat Transfer
  • Refrigeration and Air Conditioning
  • Supply Chain Management
